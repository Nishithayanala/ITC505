About the Game

The "Guess the Number" game is a simple and interactive guessing game where the player attempts to guess a randomly chosen number between 1 and 100. Each time you make a guess, you will get feedback indicating whether your guess was correct or incorrect. A new number is randomly generated for each guess to keep the game dynamic.
